Shopify Case Study

Luxury Fragrance Shopify Experience

A premium Shopify storefront designed for home, office, and car air fresheners with dynamic fragrance storytelling, custom-coded UI interactions, scent visualization, and conversion-focused cart incentives.

Crafted to Feel Like a Premium Fragrance Brand

The goal of this Shopify experience was to create more than just a product page. Every section was designed to visually communicate fragrance depth, luxury aesthetics, and interactive storytelling while maintaining fast performance and a clean shopping flow.

Custom Features

Designed to enhance engagement, storytelling, and conversion throughout the shopping experience.

Dynamic Metaobjects

Fragrance notes dynamically update for each product using Shopify Metaobjects for a scalable and premium storytelling experience.

Scent Intensity Meter

Custom-coded scent visualization that changes dynamically depending on the selected fragrance profile.

Smart Cart Incentive

A dynamic progress bar automatically unlocks a complimentary sample when cart value reaches $75.

Responsive Across Devices

Optimized for mobile-first shopping with smooth layouts, luxury spacing, and responsive product interactions.
